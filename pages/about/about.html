To calculate the weight loss, we need to know how much energy our fat tissue contains - there are approximately 7700 calories stored in every kilogram of body fat. So once you have found the number of burned calories, all you have to do is to divide by 7700 to obtain the weight loss in kilograms:

weight loss = calories/7700
